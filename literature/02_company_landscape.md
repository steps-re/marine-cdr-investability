# Marine CDR Company Landscape vs the Air-Sea Bottleneck (2024-2026)

Marketing CLAIMS separated from independently VERIFIED figures. Geographies given with lat/lon
for direct overlay on published efficiency fields. "gated" = air-sea gated; "storage" = storage-only.

## Headline finding
Independently verified, registry-issued, retired mCDR credits are ~0 before 2025. Only verified OAE credits ever:
Planetary Technologies 625.6 t (Isometric, June 2025) — credited BEFORE equilibration completed (~50% expected uptake).
All large numbers (Ebb-Microsoft 350kt, Boeing-Equatic 62kt, Gigablue 200kt, MOL-Captura 30kt) are forward offtakes.
Delivered mCDR ~= 2% of contracted.

## Registries & the equilibration question
- ISOMETRIC = effectively the only operational open-ocean mCDR registry. Protocols: OAE, Wastewater AE, Electrolytic
  Seawater Mineralization, DOCS, + Air-Sea CO2 Uptake Module. Mandates 3D bio-geochem modeling (intervention-minus-
  counterfactual flux); assumes 4.4 +/- 3.4 mo e-folding (~13 mo to 95%); does NOT credit water subducted before
  equilibrating; hard ceiling mol C removed < mol alkalinity added. Issued the only verified credits (Planetary 625.6t,
  after 14.35% uncertainty deduction). Criticism (Carbon to Sea Nov 2025): model "assumes full equilibration" yet ~60%
  of gross CDR was near-field where dissolution unconfirmed; dissolution model unpublished; discount "precautionary."
  CarbonPlan rates electrochemical OAE VCL 3/5.
- Puro.earth: terrestrial-dominant; microalgae-sinking method (Gigablue) + DAC; no OAE protocol, no equilibration accounting.
- Verra: blue carbon only; open-ocean Scope 17 under development.
- Gold Standard, Riverse, EU CRCF: terrestrial only (OAE/DOC named as future).
- Frontier Climate (BUYER, >$1B AMC): [C]Worthy "Guidance for assessing abiotic marine CDR MRV" (Fall 2024) auto-FAILS
  any project assuming seawater removal == atmospheric removal. Marine portfolio ~249kt / ~$98.6M / 12 cos; ~1,716t delivered.
- CDR.fyi: tracker not registry; "delivered" largely unaudited.

## Synthesis for paper
1. Verified ~= 0; industry runs on forward offtakes.
2. Bottleneck unpriced almost everywhere; only Isometric (3D model, eff<1 ceiling) and Frontier (pass/fail) confront it.
3. Geography maps onto efficiency: high-lat sheltered coasts (Ebb/Banyu 48N, SeaO2 52N, Planetary 44.6N = faster) vs
   subtropical/equatorial (Captura 19.7N, Equatic 1.3N, Vesta 36N, Gigablue S Pacific = slower, subduction risk).
4. Attrition real: Running Tide defunct, Brilliant Planet administration, Seafields retreated.

Caveats: CDR.fyi "delivered" != verified; Isometric near-field critique rests mainly on one review (Carbon to Sea Nov 2025);
"SUNCA"/"Sink Carbon" unverifiable; Duck olivine tonnage differs by source.
